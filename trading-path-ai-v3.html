What we assess

Your numbers.
Not a general template.

Most tools assign you to a broad category — "moderate risk" or "growth-oriented" — and stop there. TradingPath runs four variables through a structured framework and returns one specific recommendation, with every capital figure stated in actual dollars.

A $300 budget routes you differently than a $3,000 budget. Limited hours per week rules out strategies that require active position management. These aren't preferences — they're hard constraints, and the recommendation reflects them.
